Articuno

Articuno is a large bird with blue plumrage. Its colouring is darkest on its head crest and the long streaming tail. Articuno's body is coloured sky-blue,while growing lighter on its front,and its chest a lighter blue than anything. Its most destructive trait is its wings,which are said are made of ice. The eyes are a medium shade of red. Articuno is able to control ice. Thats why it is said that whenever this pokemon flies,snow starts to fall. Articuno's national number is #144.

Articuno is the strongest amongst all the 3 birds when it comes to physical strength. As it is shown in the ANIME, Articuno is able to lift one of Team Rocket's mechas with its TALONS, only its TALONS. Maye if it uses his physical stregth to the maximum,it could lift up a mountain!
